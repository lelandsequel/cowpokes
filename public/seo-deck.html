01. Technical SEO

The foundation. Google needs to read the site before it ranks it.

  • Speed: Built on Bun/Vite for near-instant loads.
  • Meta Data: Dynamic "Helmet" injection for Title/Descriptions on every page.
  • Sitemap.xml: Auto-generated map of every single page on the site.
  • Robots.txt: Instructions for Google Spiders to crawl effectively.

02. AEO

(Answer Engine Optimization)

Optimizing for the future: Voice Search & AI (ChatGPT/Perplexity).

  • Schema Markup: LocalBusiness JSON-LD tells Google exactly where you are and what you do.
  • FAQ Structure: The "Info" page is coded to answer specific questions ("Is it sanitary?", "Healing time?") so AI can extract the answers directly.
